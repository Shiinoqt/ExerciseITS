Welcome to Jollibee

Experience the joy of delicious food!

Jollibee is a Filipino multinational chain of fast food restaurants owned by Jollibee Foods Corporation. It is known for its Chickenjoy, a crispy fried chicken dish, and its unique menu that includes spaghetti, burgers, and more.

Founded in 1978, Jollibee has grown to become one of the largest fast food chains in the Philippines and has expanded internationally with locations in various countries.
